Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, creamy mascarpone filling, and a dusting of rich cocoa powder. Light yet indulgent, it's the perfect balance of sweetness and espresso flavor.

Ingredients

  • 300g of Ladyfingers
  • 500g of mascarpone cheese
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 100g of granulated sugar
  • 300 ml of espresso coffee
  • 2 tbsp of Marsala Wine
  • unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Make the coffee & add 2 tbsp of Marsala wine. Set aside and cool.
  2. Separate eggs & whip egg whites until stiff.
  3. Beat the egg yolks with sugar until light and smooth.
  4. Pour mascarpone cheese into a bowl and work it with a spoon to make it softer. When the yolks are ready add the mascarpone cheese.
  5. Slowly whip the mascarpone cream for 2 to 3 minutes. Now add the stiffly beaten egg whites.
  6. Mix with a wooden spoon, from bottom up. Mix slowly until smooth and creamy.
  7. Now let's prepare the layers of ladyfingers and mascarpone cream. You can make 2 or more layers, depending on the width and depth of your pan.
  8. Dip the ladyfingers quickly (1 or 2 seconds) into the coffee. Then arrange the ladyfingers in the casserole of your liking.
  9. Arrange them so that they cover the bottom of the casserole. Then spread the mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers.
  10. Add another layer of ladyfingers and then top with more mascarpone cream. If you are making the last layer, spread the mascarpone cream generously.
  11. Finally, sprinkle with cocoa powder. You can even add dark chocolate chips, if you like. Allow to rest 3 hours in the refrigerator before serving.
